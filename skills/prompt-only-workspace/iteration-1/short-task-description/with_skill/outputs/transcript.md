```
**Task:** Draft an email asking coauthors to review the latest draft of a joint paper on firm dynamics before a meeting next Tuesday (April 7, 2026).

**Context:**
- Sender is a research economist at the Federal Reserve Bank of Atlanta
- The coauthors are fellow academics/researchers familiar with the project

- Keep it to 4-6 sentences
- Friendly but professional tone
- Mention that the meeting is next Tuesday and ask them to review before then

- Do not include pleasantries beyond a brief greeting
- Sign off with first name only

**Output format:** A ready-to-send email with subject line, greeting, body, and sign-off.
```
